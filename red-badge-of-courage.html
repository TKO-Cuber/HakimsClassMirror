Red Badge of Courage

From AmericanLiterature.com, "Follow the trials and tribulations of Henry Fleming, a recruit in the American Civil War in this impressionistic novel by American writer Stephen Crane. Considered one of the most influential war stories ever written, 'The Red Badge of Courage' was published in 1895, a full thirty years after the American Civil War ended. Although Stephen Crane was born after the war and never participated in battle himself, he was highly praised by the Civil War veterans for having capture a realistic impression of their actual battlefield experiences and emotions."
