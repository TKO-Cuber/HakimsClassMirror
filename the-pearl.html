Pearl, An english poem of the 14th century (translated)

In Kino's journey to safeguard the pearl, he becomes entangled in a spiritual struggle, one similar to that of the anonymous poet in the 14th century poem, Pearl. The poem, originally written in medieval English, tells of the unfortunate death of the poet's two-year-old daughter and his attempt to accept the loss. It's about "realizing the limits of human will and the confines of human consciousness."

Read the poem and relate it to Kino's own realization of the limits of human will.
