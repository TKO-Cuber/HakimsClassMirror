What is Cranium Club?

          The Cranium Club is for students interested in going "above and beyond" the learning done in the classroom. Students who are interested in stretching their brains can work on Cranium Club questions during free time at home or at school.  
          When you feel you have mastered the answer(s) to a question, write the question and record your answer in a notebook or a sheet of paper and turn it in on Friday.  If you do not pass the first time, you can try again another day.
          You can become a 1-star member by answering one question correctly, or keep going to really build those brain wrinkles and work your way all the way up to a 126-star member! Every 20 stars earns you extra credit in social studies.
